> [!NOTE] Key Message
> 2020 ACC/AHA VHD Guideline は **狭小弁輪・AAE・PPM を明示的に取り上げる**。Figure 3 注釈で "**With a small aortic annulus or aorta, a surgical annulus-enlarging procedure may be needed to allow placement of a larger prosthesis and avoid patient–prosthesis mismatch**" と AAE を SAVR の adjunct として位置付け (Class なし、本文 narrative 推奨)。SAVR vs TAVI 選択: **<65 y → SAVR (Class I A)**, **65–80 y → SAVR or TF-TAVI (Class I A, shared decision)**, **>80 y or LE <10 y → TF-TAVI 優先 (Class I A)**。Table 24 (Mechanical vs Bioprosthetic) で "**Small aortic root size for AVR (may preclude ViV)**" と "**TAVI valves have larger effective orifice areas for smaller valve sizes (avoid PPM)**" を bioprosthesis を支持する factor として明記。AAE は Class/LOE 付与なしも、**Comprehensive Valve Center で行うべき手技** (Section 2 referral) として institutional 要件に組み込まれる。

## 1. 背景

2020年 ACC/AHA は 2014年版を全面改訂し VHD 全般 (AS, AR, MS, MR, TR, prosthetic valve) を網羅。AS では TAVI の low-risk extension (PARTNER 3, Evolut Low Risk) を反映し、SAVR vs TAVI の age cutoff 推奨を初めて明文化。Section 11 (prosthetic valves) で **valve choice / PPM / ViV** を統合的に扱う。

## 2. 対象範囲

- 成人 VHD 全般 (本MDでは AS / AVR 関連箇所のみ抽出)
- AS staging (A, B, C1/C2, D1/D2/D3) を維持
- Heart Valve Team (Multidisciplinary) を Class I で要請、Comprehensive Valve Center (Level 2) は **複雑手技 (Ross, AAE, multivalve, redo, complex MV repair)** を担う

## 3. ガイドライン構成

- 1. Introduction / 2. General Principles (Heart Team, Risk Assessment)
- 3. AS (3.2.3 Timing of Intervention, 3.2.4 Choice of Intervention)
  - 3.2.4.1 Mechanical vs Bioprosthetic
  - 3.2.4.2 SAVR vs TAVI
- 4–10. その他弁
- 11. Prosthetic Valves (11.8 Stenosis, ViV)

## 4. 主要推奨

### 4.1 AVR Timing (Section 3.2.3)

| 推奨 | Class | LOE | 原文要旨 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Symptomatic severe high-gradient AS (Stage D1, exertional dyspnea/HF/angina/syncope) → AVR | **I** | **A** | "AVR is indicated" |
| Asymptomatic severe AS + LVEF <50% (C2) → AVR | I | B-NR | "AVR is indicated" |
| Asymptomatic severe AS (C1) + cardiac surgery for other indications → AVR | I | B-NR | "AVR is indicated" |
| Symptomatic low-flow low-gradient AS + LVEF↓ (D2) → AVR | I | B-NR | "AVR is recommended" |
| Symptomatic LFLG-AS + normal EF (D3, AS most likely cause) → AVR | I | B-NR | "AVR is recommended" |
| Asymp C1 + low risk + abnormal ETT → AVR | IIa | B-NR | "AVR is reasonable" |
| Asymp very severe (V ≥5 m/s) + low risk → AVR | IIa | B-NR | "AVR is reasonable" |
| Asymp C1 + low risk + BNP >3× normal → AVR | IIa | B-NR | "AVR is reasonable" |
| Asymp C1 + rapid progression (ΔV ≥0.3 m/s/yr) → AVR | IIa | B-NR | "AVR is reasonable" |
| Asymp C1 + LVEF 漸減 <60% → AVR | IIb | C-EO | "may be considered" |
| Moderate AS + other cardiac surgery → AVR | IIb | C-EO | "may be considered" |

### 4.2 SAVR vs TAVI (Section 3.2.4.2)

| 推奨 | Class | LOE | 原文 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **<65 y or LE >20 y → SAVR** | **I** | **A** | "SAVR is recommended" |
| **65–80 y + no TF anatomic contraindication → SAVR or TF-TAVI (shared decision)** | **I** | **A** | "either SAVR or transfemoral TAVI is recommended" |
| **>80 y or younger with LE <10 y + no TF contraindication → TF-TAVI** | **I** | **A** | "transfemoral TAVI is recommended in preference to SAVR" |
| Asymp severe AS + EF<50% ≤80 y → same as symp (1–3) | I | B-NR | — |
| Asymp severe AS + abnormal exercise/very severe/rapid prog/elevated BNP → **SAVR over TAVI** | I | A | "SAVR is recommended in preference to TAVI" |
| Bioprosthesis preferred but anatomy unsuitable for TF-TAVI → SAVR | I | A | — |
| Symp severe AS + high/prohibitive risk + post-TAVI survival >12 mo → TAVI | I | A | — |
| Critically ill + bridge → percutaneous aortic balloon dilation | IIb | C-EO | "may be considered" |

### 4.3 Mechanical vs Bioprosthetic (Section 3.2.4.1)

| 推奨 | Class | LOE | 原文 |
|---|---|---|---|
| <50 y + no contraindication to anticoag → **mechanical valve** preferred | I | B-NR | (Recommendation 2 in Section 3.2.4.1) |
| >65 y → bioprosthetic reasonable (avoid VKA risk) | IIa | — | "reasonable to use a bioprosthetic valve in patients >65 y" |
| 50–65 y → shared decision (uncertainty) | — | — | — |
| Ross procedure → Comprehensive Valve Center, selected younger patients | IIb | — | — |

### 4.4 狭小弁輪・AAE への明示的言及 (Figure 3 注釈 / Table 24)

**Figure 3 dagger 注 (line 4242-4243, AVR 選択フローの注釈)**:
> "**A large aortic annulus may not be suitable for currently available transcatheter valve sizes. With a small aortic annulus or aorta, a surgical annulus-enlarging procedure may be needed to allow placement of a larger prosthesis and avoid patient–prosthesis mismatch.**"

**Table 24 (Choice of Mechanical vs Bioprosthetic, line ~9100)** — bioprosthesis を支持する factor として:
> "**Small aortic root size for AVR (may preclude ViV procedure in future)**"
> "**TAVI valves have larger effective orifice areas for smaller valve sizes (avoid patient–prosthesis mismatch)**"

**Section 2 / Table 5 (Comprehensive Valve Center 要件)** — Comprehensive Valve Center で行うべき手技として:
> "**Root enlargement with AVR**" (Aortic root procedures for aneurysmal disease, Concomitant septal myectomy with AVR と並列)

### 4.5 ViV / PPM (Section 11.8.2)

| 推奨 | Class | LOE | 原文 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Symptomatic severe bioprosthetic stenosis → repeat surgery unless high/prohibitive risk | I | B-NR | "repeat surgical intervention is indicated" |
| Severe bioprosthetic AS + high/prohibitive risk → **transcatheter ViV at Comprehensive Valve Center** | IIa | B-NR | "transcatheter ViV procedure is reasonable" |
| Bioprosthetic stenosis + suspected thrombus → VKA | IIa | B-NR | "oral anticoagulation with a VKA is reasonable" |

Section 11.8 synopsis:
> "Cumulative survival rates are higher with reoperative AVR than with transcatheter ViV treatment for prosthetic valve stenosis, and **a surgical approach is associated with a reduced incidence of patient–prosthesis mismatch**, reduced incidence of paravalvular leak, and lower aortic valve gradients."

## 5. 議論ポイント

- **AAE は Class/LOE 付与なし** — narrative 推奨と institutional 要件のみ。**STS expert consensus** は paper_list.md 警告通り存在せず、ACC/AHA の図注釈が唯一の "公式" 推奨。
- TAVI を狭小弁輪で支持する論理: Figure 3 注釈 + Table 24 "TAVI valves have larger EOA for smaller valve sizes" は **TAVI を狭小弁輪 PPM 回避の手段** として明示的に位置付ける。
- 同時に Table 24 で "Small aortic root size (may preclude ViV)" を **bioprosthesis を支持する factor** (将来の ViV を可能にする) と書いており、若年 bio 弁選択時に **AAE で十分大きい弁を入れることが ViV 余地を作る** という論理を示唆。
- LE-based age cutoff (65/80) は SAVR vs TAVI 選択の最大の改変点。

## 6. 本研究RQへの含意

- **Primary RQ (AAE-SAVR vs no AAE vs TAVI)**:
  - 本ガイドラインは **狭小弁輪に対し AAE を行う合理的選択肢として narrative 推奨**。Class/LOE はないが、Figure 3 注釈の表現は **"may be needed to ... avoid PPM"** と明示。
  - 同時に **狭小弁輪 + 高齢 (>80 y) は TAVI 優先 (Class I A)**。
  - 65–80 y で狭小弁輪は **TAVI の large EOA 利点が SAVR より大きい可能性** を Table 24 が示唆 → AAE-SAVR vs TAVI が真の clinical equipoise 領域。
- **Secondary RQ (PPM/regression/予後)**:
  - PPM 定義は Section 11.8 で narrative 記載 (Pibarot cutoff)。
  - "PPM is associated with high transvalvular gradient, persistent LV hypertrophy, increased rate of cardiac events" と明記。
  - **ViV vs redo SAVR**: redo SAVR の方が PPM 少 → 若年 bio 選択時に **AAE で初回弁を大きく入れる戦略の long-term rationale** を提供。
- **Tertiary RQ (Asian/小体格)**:
  - **日本人小体格特異的言及なし**。米国データに基づく。
  - LE-based cutoff は本邦の長寿命を考慮すると 80 y は早すぎる可能性 (JCS と乖離する点)。
- **AAE 代替議論 (sutureless)**: Sutureless valve への明示的言及はほぼなし (本ガイドラインの ranged 弁選択枠で扱われる程度)。

## 7. 限界

- 米国データに偏重、Asian/小体格 specific 推奨なし。
- AAE に Class/LOE が付与されていない → 強い evidence ではなく "narrative consensus"。
- TAVI durability data <5 y 時点での age cutoff、後の改訂で変動余地。
- SURD (sutureless) の位置付けが弱い。
